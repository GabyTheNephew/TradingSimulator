Beginner's Guide to the Chart

Understanding the Visuals

Green Candle: The price went up. The bottom of the box is the start price, the top is the finish price.
Red Candle: The price went down. The top of the box is the start price, the bottom is the finish price.
Green Volume: More people were buying than selling during this time. Buying interest is strong.
Red Volume: More people were selling than buying. There is high selling pressure.

Chart Helpers & Axes

$

📅
Time Axis (Bottom): Shows when the price movement happened (Date and Time).
SMA (Orange): A smooth average line that helps you ignore chaotic jumps and see the real direction.
Bollinger Area: A visual 'highway'. If the price hits the top or bottom edges, it might turn back soon.
